Impact of Gaming on Youth

A data-driven analysis of how gaming influences youth behavior, academic focus, and physical health.

Abstract & Summary

This research studies the psychological, academic, and physical impact of video gaming on youth aged 13–21. Our findings suggest that while moderate gaming (1-2 hours) improves spatial awareness and problem-solving, excessive gaming (4+ hours) correlates with a significant decline in sleep quality and attention span.
