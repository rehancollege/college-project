Gaming Awareness & Healthy Practices

Balancing virtual worlds and real-life wellness

Behavior Guidelines

✔ POSITIVE HABITS

  • Set a daily time limit for gaming.
  • Take a 10-minute break every hour.
  • Maintain ergonomic posture.

❌ RISK FACTORS

  • Playing late at night regularly.
  • Skipping meals or homework.
  • Aggressive online interactions.

Healthy Gaming Flowchart

Success Roadmap

1
Plan Your Time Decide your session length before you power up.
2
Responsibilities First Complete homework and chores as 'priority quests'.
3
Stay Hydrated Drink water regularly to maintain focus and energy.
4
Weekly Reflection Check if gaming is affecting your sleep or mood.

Security Protocol

  • Privacy First: Never share passwords, addresses, or real names.
  • Report Toxicity: Use in-game tools to report harassment immediately.
  • Two-Factor Auth: Enable 2FA on all gaming accounts for safety.
  • Verified Sources: Only install games from official platforms.

Physical Wellness

Optimize your "biological hardware" for better performance.

  • 20-20-20 Rule: Every 20 mins, look 20 feet away for 20 seconds.
  • Ergonomic Setup: Keep your screen at eye level to avoid neck strain.
  • Active Recovery: Stand up and stretch during loading screens.

Social Balance

Community Connection

Prioritize playing with friends or family over strictly solo or stranger-based matchmaking.

The "Dinner Table" Rule

Never let a match interrupt face-to-face social time or shared family meals.

Digital Sportsmanship

Encourage teammates and stay positive; toxic behavior leads to increased stress levels.
