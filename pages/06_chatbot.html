Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is digital wellness?

Digital wellness means maintaining a healthy balance between technology use and physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

2. How much gaming is considered safe?

3. Can gaming affect academic performance?

Yes, excessive gaming can reduce focus, study time, and sleep quality, leading to poor academic outcomes.

4. Does gaming impact mental health?

Overuse of games may increase stress, anxiety, and social isolation in some individuals.

5. What are the positive effects of gaming?

Moderate gaming can improve problem-solving skills, hand-eye coordination, and teamwork abilities.

6. What is gaming addiction?

Gaming addiction is a condition where a person loses control over gaming habits, affecting daily responsibilities and relationships.

7. How does late-night gaming affect sleep?

Late-night gaming exposes users to blue light, which disrupts melatonin production and disturbs sleep cycles.

8. Can gaming affect physical health?

Long hours of sitting while gaming may cause eye strain, poor posture, headaches, and reduced physical activity.

9. How can students maintain digital balance?

Students can set time limits, prioritize studies, take regular breaks, and engage in outdoor activities.

10. Why is digital wellness important?

Digital wellness helps individuals stay productive, healthy, and socially connected while enjoying technology responsibly.
