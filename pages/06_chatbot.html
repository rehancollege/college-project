Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is digital wellness?

Digital wellness means maintaining a healthy balance between technology use and physical, mental, and emotional well-being.

3. Can gaming affect academic performance?

Yes, excessive gaming can reduce focus, study time, and sleep quality, leading to poor academic outcomes.

4. Does gaming impact mental health?

Overuse of games may increase stress, anxiety, and social isolation in some individuals.

5. Are there positive effects of gaming?

Yes. Moderate gaming can improve problem-solving skills, coordination, and teamwork.

6. How does gaming affect sleep?

Late-night gaming can disturb sleep cycles, leading to fatigue and reduced concentration.

7. What are signs of gaming addiction?

Loss of control, neglecting responsibilities, irritability, and reduced social interaction.

8. Can parents control gaming habits?

Yes. Setting time limits and encouraging outdoor activities helps maintain balance.

9. Is online gaming safe for youth?

With proper supervision and privacy settings, online gaming can be safe.

10. How can students maintain healthy gaming habits?

By following schedules, taking breaks, and prioritizing studies and health.
